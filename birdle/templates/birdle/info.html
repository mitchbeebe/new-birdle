Welcome to Birdle!


How to Play

You get six guesses to identify the bird of the day. If you guess incorrectly, but your guess shares the order, family, or genus (taxonomic rank) with the species of the day, the tiles below will turn green. Good luck!


About Me

My name is Mitch (@mitchbeebe on most platforms). I'm the creator of Birdle. I'm neither a birder nor web developer. I jokingly threw the name 'Birdle' out there to a friend that is an avid birder and also very into all -dle games (Wordle, Worldle, Flagle, Globle, to name a few). He proceeded to ask me about development progress daily until I caved and hacked this together.


I'm the sole developer and use my own money to keep Birdle running. Consider donating if you like the game.
